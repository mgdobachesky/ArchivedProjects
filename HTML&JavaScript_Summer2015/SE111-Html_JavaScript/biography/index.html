Bio

Hello everyone, my name is Michael Dobachesky and I am from Plainville, Massachusetts. I was raised in Plainville and have gone through it's school system, and I also did some schooling with Berklee Online. I love playing music; I write, produce, and perform. My main instrument is the paino and my secondary instrument is the drums. I also love running, and anything else that has to do with fitness. One of my goals is to run a marathon. I look forward to getting to know you all!
